Hermes Library Webpage's Privacy Policy

Last updated: 06 August 2023

  1. Introduction

    Welcome to the Hermes Library's website. We value our users' privacy and are dedicated to preserving it through adherence to this policy.

    This policy explains the sorts of information we may collect from you or that you may supply when visiting our website, as well as our procedures for collecting, using, storing, and disclosing that information.

  2. Information We Collect and How We Collect It

    We gather numerous sorts of information from and about our website visitors, including:

    • We get it directly from you when you agree and provide it to us.
    • As you explore the site, this happens automatically.
      Automatically gathered information may include use details, IP addresses, and information obtained through cookies.
  3. Information You Provide to Us

    We may collect the following types of information on or via our website:

    • Personal Data: While using our website, we may request that you give us with personally identifying information that will allow us to contact you. Your email address, name, and phone number are all examples of personally identifiable information.
  4. How We Will Use Your Data

    We utilize the following information about you that we collect or that you supply to us, including any personal data:

    • To introduce you to our website and its contents.
    • To send you the books you've requested.
    • To keep you informed of any changes to our website or any items we sell or supply.
    • With your permission, for any other reason.
  5. Information Dissemination

    We do not sell, trade, or rent personal identifiable information about our users to third parties. We may share general aggregated demographic information about visitors and users that is not linked to any personal identifiable information with our business partners, trusted affiliates, and advertising.
